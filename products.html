Your Loan

ACC uses Advantedge technology to help with the processing of your loan. Advantedge is part of the National Australia Bank Group (NAB) and is Australia’s leading wholesale funder and distributor of white-label home loans. Advantedge supports mortgage brokers and mortgage managers with end-to-end loan processing and administration support. From pre- to post-settlement stages, Advantedge also gives brokers access to a personalised lending team that prides itself on excellent service.

The Process - In Steps

  • 1. Get in Touch

    We will talk to you about your financial situation, goals and lending needs.

  • 2. Calculate Your Borrowing Power

    We will do the math and let you know how much you can borrow.

  • 3. Compare and Find a Lender

    We will compare various home loan options from Australian lenders and find the perfect fit for your needs. We’ll also explain the requirements, terms and conditions of each option.

  • 4. Submit an Application

    ACC will guide you through the process of preparing your application. Once submitted, we will liase with the lender on your behalf and get it conditionally approved as soon as possible.

  • 5. Get Approval and Prepare for Settlement

    After the lender is satisfied and you have been unconditionally approved, ACC will help you understand the loan documents and get ready for settlement.
